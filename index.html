Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Karl Keshavarzi!

My journey with computers started when I was 10 years old, taking apart my family's old desktop just to see how it worked. That curiosity never left me, and over the years, it grew into a genuine passion for building systems, regardless of its performance. Now, I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Applied Science in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo!

After spending a couple of years tinkering with computers on a limited budget, I realized I wanted to turn my passion into something more. I asked my parents for a small loan to buy quality components, built my first custom system, and sold it for a modest profit. That first successful build allowed me to pay back my parents and reinvest in better parts, and just like that, my hobby became a growing business.

But it wasn't just about the profit. Every time I handed over a completed build and saw the excitement on someone's face when they powered it on for the first time, I knew I had found something special. I wanted to share that same passion and excitement that got me hooked on computers in the first place.

That's why I founded KesTech Systems. Here, every build is personal. Whether you're a gamer chasing the highest frame rates, a content creator needing reliable performance, or someone who just wants a computer that works perfectly for years to come, I'm here to make it happen.
